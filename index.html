Tatum Glen
Assisted Living Home

Delivering reliable and professional care built on 25 years of hands-on experience in supporting seniors.

Family-Owned

We are a family-owned assisted living home offering hands-on, personalized care in a warm, home-like environment. Each resident receives individual attention, including customized care plans and personalized meals based on preferences and dietary needs.

Exceptional Medical Support

Our home provides comprehensive medical care on-site. House doctors visit regularly, and services such as blood work and X-rays are completed at home. All care is coordinated in-house, with a nearby hospital for additional support when needed.

Daily Activities & Wellness

We offer daily activities to keep residents active and engaged, including stationary bicycle exercise, seated exercises, bingo games, and movie time. When the weather is comfortable, we enjoy short walks around the block, and for those who are able, outings to restaurants and the mall for a fun change of scenery.

24-Hour Support

Care and supervision are available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. Trained staff are always on-site to assist with daily needs, emergencies, and overnight care. The owner lives directly across the street, providing immediate support, constant oversight, and added peace of mind at all times.

about us

Our Home

Our home is designed to feel warm, safe, and familiar. With a balanced size and a family-style setting, residents enjoy comfort, personal space, and a true sense of home.

Our Care team

As a family-run assisted living home, our staff provides hands-on, personalized care in a warm and supportive environment. We take the time to understand each resident’s individual needs, routines, and preferences. With 24/7 staff availability, families can feel confident knowing care and support are always close by.
